About me

I am originally from a small town in Northern New Mexico. When I tell
people I am from New Mexico, I usually get either one of two answers:
"Wow, you're the first person I've ever met from NM," or "so how do you
like breaking bad?" It's funny, because it's almost similar to the kind of
answers I get when I tell people I am learning how to code. I currently
work in the art world at a museum. When I explain to people that I am
enrolled in this course, it usually elicts a confused response.

In addition to making people confused, coding, and being around art, I also like
to spend my free time eating icecream, hiking, reading, and the occational
Netflix binge.
